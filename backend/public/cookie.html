Cookie Policy and Usage

A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. We use cookies to personalise content and ads, to provide social media features and to analyse our traffic. We also share information about your use of our site with our social media, advertising and analytics partners who may combine it with other information that you’ve provided to them or that they’ve collected from your use of their services. Currently, cookies are utilized by GoWebSurveys within the following four primary categories or uses:

(1) Necessary cookies help make a website usable by enabling basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies. Name: PHPSESSID

(2) Statistic cookies help website owners to understand how visitors interact with websites by collecting and reporting information anonymously. e.g. _ga, _gid and _gat which are Google Analytics Cookies

(3) targeting cookies which are utilized to measure webviewing for market research purposes.
