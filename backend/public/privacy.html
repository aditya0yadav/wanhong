In connection with your registration with the Panel, we will collect personal information, including without limitation your first and last name, email address, gender, date of birth, address, zip code and other location information as described below.

As a member of the Panel, you may also provide us with additional information which helps us target you with appropriate survey opportunities, including without limitation your purchasing history and preferences, voting history and political opinions, past diagnoses, other medical information, and demographic information such as your age, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, race or ethnicity, religious beliefs, parental status, marital status, education, and professional credentials.

All such personal information is provided by you to us voluntarily. In all the above instances you will know what personal information you provide because you will provide it to us directly.

3.2 In Connection with Surveys

In addition to information collected in connection with your registration and screening surveys available in connection with your account profile, we or our clients or partners may collect additional personal information in connection with individual surveys which are routed to you.

3.3 Location Information

During the sign-up process, we may collect location information to determine your geographical location. This information is collected using MaxMind's geo-location technology and is used solely for verification and fraud prevention purposes.

We understand that users may be traveling and accessing our services from different locations. Our geo-location technology is designed to provide accurate information based on your current location at the time of registration. If you are traveling and using a different location than your home country, our technology may detect that location. However, we do not manually select the location; it is automatically determined by our system based on available data. We do not collect personal travel details or track users beyond their initial registration.

Web log data includes the browser type you're using and traffic to and from our Site to determine trends, administer the Site, track user movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.
